Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

📍 FindMyPeanut is a community lost & found network for pets.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly:

  • Account information — email address when you sign in via magic link or Google OAuth
  • Pet reports — pet description, photos, and last known location you submit
  • Sightings — location, time, and photos when you report seeing a pet
  • Profile information — optional display name and contact preferences

We also collect limited technical information automatically:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App usage patterns to improve the service
  • Approximate location (only when you grant permission)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To display lost and found pet reports on the community map
  • To send notifications when someone spots your pet or reports one near you
  • To match lost pets with found pet reports and nearby sightings
  • To verify shelter and rescue organization accounts
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs

3. Location Data

Location is used only to show nearby reports and to attach a location to sightings you submit. We apply coordinate fuzzing (~100 meters) to all public report locations to protect the privacy of pet owners. Exact coordinates are never shown publicly.

We do not track your location in the background. Location is only accessed when you actively use the app.

4. Anonymous Reporting

You can submit lost or found pet reports without creating an account. Anonymous reports are stored without any personally identifiable information. If you later create an account, you may claim your anonymous reports.

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Publicly — pet reports and sightings are visible to other users to help find pets
  • Service providers — we use Supabase for database and authentication, and Stadia Maps for mapping. These providers process data only as necessary to provide their services.
  • Legal requirements — if required by law or to protect the safety of users

6. Data Retention

Pet reports remain active until you mark them resolved or delete them. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Settings page in the app.

7. Children's Privacy

FindMyPeanut is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS), row-level security on our database, and secure authentication. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

9. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us or using the account deletion feature in the app.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact us at findmypeanut@gmail.com
